# Optimism & Ethereum Bridge Tutorial

Bridge your ERC-20 tokens created on exercise 5 from Sepolia testnet to Optimism Sepolia. The step-by-step gruide is [available](https://docs.optimism.io/builders/app-developers/tutorials/cross-dom-bridge-erc20).

## What to do to get points
You need to send a transaction going from L1 to L2 (ETH Sepolia to OP Sepolia) and withing that transaction use your own ERC-20 tokens you created before.
* To get 4 points, your exerciser needs to check that your wallet has the same ERC-20 token on both networks. To do that, everything will be checked on-chain, therefore send your exerciser your wallet address on Sepolia testnet.
